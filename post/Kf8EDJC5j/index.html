EME6414 Week 9 #2 Interest-driven Community

07/15/2023

I am a fan of traditional Beijing Opera, and recently I discovered that the video-based social media platform bilibili.com has fostered a thriving community centered around Beijing Opera. This is particularly noteworthy considering the declining attendance of younger generations at live opera and musical shows, a trend also observed in the United States.

The question arises: how does a prosperous community grow in such circumstances? Building a prosperous community around traditional art forms requires various factors coming together:

Accessibility: Online platforms like bilibili.com provide easy access to content related to Beijing Opera. This allows enthusiasts, including younger generations, to explore and engage with the art form conveniently from the comfort of their own homes. By removing barriers to access, online platforms help broaden the reach of traditional art forms.

Community Engagement: A prosperous community thrives on active participation and engagement. Online platforms facilitate interaction and collaboration among enthusiasts, creating spaces for discussions, sharing knowledge, and fostering a sense of belonging. Through features like comments, forums, and user-generated content, individuals can connect, learn from each other, and deepen their passion for Beijing Opera.

Education and Awareness: Nurturing a prosperous community involves educating and raising awareness about the art form. Online platforms can serve as educational resources, providing historical context, explanations of performance techniques, and behind-the-scenes insights. By promoting understanding and appreciation, these platforms help attract newcomers and cultivate a new generation of Beijing Opera enthusiasts.

Promotion and Innovation: To sustain a prosperous community, it is crucial to adapt to the changing preferences and interests of younger generations. Online platforms can be utilized to showcase Beijing Opera in innovative ways, such as incorporating modern elements, collaborating with other art forms, or experimenting with digital media. By embracing new approaches, traditional art forms can remain relevant and capture the interest of younger audiences.
